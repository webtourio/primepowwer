Our Partnership with Matrix Cleaning Systems

DEMO TEXTSince 2015, PrimPower has been a proud authorized distributor of Matrix Cleaning Systems equipment. This strategic partnership has allowed us to offer our clients access to some of the most innovative and reliable cleaning machines available in the market. Matrix Cleaning Systems is renowned for their cutting-edge technology and robust engineering. We've built our foundation on distributing their high-performance equipment, which has earned the trust of professional cleaning operations across the UK. From their industrial floor scrubbers to advanced pressure washers, Matrix machines are synonymous with reliability and efficiency.

Our mission is simple — deliver powerful cleaning solutions that improve efficiency, safety, and long-term performance for our clients.
